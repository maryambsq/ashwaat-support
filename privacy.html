Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

At Ashwaat, we value your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data when you use the Ashwaat app.

Data We Collect

Ashwaat does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

To provide lap tracking functionality, Ashwaat accesses:

  • Location data (GPS) – used only on your device to detect your laps during Tawaaf and Sa’i.
  • Motion data – used only on your device to improve accuracy.

This information is never sent to our servers, shared, or stored externally.

How We Use Your Data

Location and motion data are used in real-time to detect and confirm lap progress during rituals. All processing happens locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Ashwaat uses map data from OpenStreetMap, a community-driven public mapping project. Your location may be used to request reference data (e.g. landmarks or coordinates) from OpenStreetMap servers.

This map data is accessed in real-time to enhance accuracy for lap tracking. Ashwaat does not store or share your location with OpenStreetMap or any other service.

Map data © OpenStreetMap contributors. Licensed under the Open Database License (ODbL).

Children’s Privacy

Ashwaat is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if needed. If significant changes are made, we will update this page with the new version and date.
